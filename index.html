    Signature profile was incorporated in 2012 with a vision of producing good quality and yet affordable clothing firstly to Nigerians, African then the world. The label owner is appalled Africa has no Significant representation in the fashion space yet we have about a billion people population. The need to fight for the African space in the global market space has kept the brand in consistent motion to do more and this gave birth to the X-sporta by sp collection which is majorly targeted at meeting the clothing needs of the youths (these are people who constitute a vast majority of Africa's population. X-sporta by Sp promotes African Pop culture and we know that that culture is taking over the world with our entertainment industry blazing the trail. Clothing is closely knitted with entertainment as since one has grown the other must follow.

    Have you ever wondered what" Signatureprofile "really means? Does it not sound tautological ? The name is a deliberate emphasis on the motive.
    Signature: distinctive characteristic indicative of identity. Profile: Character; totality of related characteristics; signature. The emphasis is on the fact that our work is extremely loud and announces itself even in a crowd.
    Signature profile has at the moment 4 sub divisions i.e collections. They are premium, classic, X-sporta and Jean Afrique. Premium is into production of high end KAFTANS, bubus and other native wears. Classic produces the day to day African tradional clothes commonly referred to as casuals. X-sporta deals with production of sport wears, t-shirts, uniforms, including shirts, pants, overalls etc. Jean Afrique :, produces all manner of Jean clothing from Jean pants, jackets, shorts, dresses etc. However, what stands Jean Afrique is that everything done under the name has a touch of African- Ness. Signature profile is a Afro- centric, clothing line and we tell the African story proudly.
